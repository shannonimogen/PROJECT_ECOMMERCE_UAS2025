NOW YOU CAN USE DISCORD APP TROUGH PS 5

The collaboration between PlayStation 5 and Discord has brought an exciting new level of connectivity for gamers. With this integration, players can now easily connect their PlayStation Network accounts to Discord, allowing them to see what their friends are playing, join voice chats, and share gaming experiences in real-time. This partnership enhances the social aspect of gaming, making it easier to communicate with friends across platforms while playing on the PS5. The seamless integration also brings added convenience, enabling players to invite friends to join their game sessions with just a few clicks. This collaboration marks a significant step forward in enhancing the overall gaming experience and fostering a more connected gaming community.
